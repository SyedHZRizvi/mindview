Notes. Students and Teachers can't access Manage Users — to change their own name or password, ask an Administrator or Super User to edit them. Nobody (not even a Super User) can change their own role or delete their own account, to prevent accidental lock-out. Content protection (copy / right-click / print / drag-save) is only relaxed for Super Users; everyone else sees the site read-only.
