Chapters

Chapter 1
Unit 1: Foundations of Critical Reading & Analysis
Literary devices, close reading of short fiction and poetry, theme & motif tracing, annotating texts, paraphrase vs analysis
Chapter 2
Unit 2: The Essay — Process & Forms
The writing process, thesis construction, expository / persuasive / literary-analysis essays, MLA citation, revision and editing
Chapter 3
Unit 3: Shakespearean Tragedy — Hamlet
Elizabethan context, soliloquy and dramatic conventions, the tragic hero, close reading of key scenes, performance analysis
Chapter 4
Unit 4: Canadian Voices in Literature
Canadian short fiction and poetry, Indigenous storytelling, post-colonial perspectives, place and identity in CanLit, Munro / Atwood / King
Chapter 5
Unit 5: Independent Study Unit (ISU)
Selecting a novel of literary merit, research and synthesis, sustained literary-analysis essay (1500–2000 words), oral defence
Chapter 6
Unit 6: Media Literacy & Critical Analysis
Decoding media texts, news vs opinion, advertising rhetoric, social-media epistemology, creating an original media text
